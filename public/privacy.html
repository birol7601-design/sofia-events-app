Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

For questions: sofiabuzzed@gmail.com

Who we are

SofiaBuzz is an events discovery platform for Sofia, Bulgaria. We help locals find concerts, festivals, and nightlife events, and help organizers reach their audience. Our contact email is sofiabuzzed@gmail.com.

What data we collect

When you register: your username, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash — we never store your actual password). Optionally: a short bio you choose to add. When you use the app: events you save or mark as attending (stored to power your profile). If you opt in: your email for event recommendations. We do not collect location data, payment card details, or any data from your device beyond what you voluntarily provide.

How we use your data

To create and manage your account. To show you your saved events and attending list. To let other users find your public profile (only if you enable this). To send you event recommendations if you opted in — you can unsubscribe anytime by emailing sofiabuzzed@gmail.com. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

Who sees your data

Your email is never shown publicly. Your username and bio are visible to other users only if you set your profile to public. Your saved and attending events are visible to other users only if your profile is public. Organizers cannot see which users saved or attended their events — only aggregate counts are available.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored on secured cloud servers in the EU (Railway.app infrastructure). Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text. We use JWT tokens for authentication — these expire after 7 days.

Your rights

You can edit or delete your bio at any time from your profile page. You can switch your profile between public and private at any time. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing sofiabuzzed@gmail.com — we will process this within 14 days. Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

Cookies and local storage

SofiaBuzz uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. No tracking cookies are used. No third-party analytics or advertising scripts are loaded.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app grows. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email.
